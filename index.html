      Data & methodology

      • Grades — official UCSD grade distributions, 2015–2026, aggregated per course × professor across quarters. The bar shows the full spread A/B/C/D/F, plus W (withdraw) and P/NP; Avg GPA is the historical mean.
      • Offered this term — matched against the current schedule snapshot ( course codes; codes only — the snapshot has no instructor names). An Offered tag means the course code is in the schedule, so it runs this term — but we do not know which professor teaches it. Names shown are past instructors of that course.
      • RMP — RateMyProfessors (school 1079), name-matched (~80% hit). Difficulty lower is easier; again is would-take-again %. Blank means no match.
      • Grad backfill — courses with no grade data (mostly 200+) are filled in reverse from professors' RMP course history, shown as RMP only. Those RMP scores are the professor's overall rating, not specific to that course.
      • Caveat — history is a guide, not a guarantee; instructors and grading policies change. Fewer times taught = less reliable (use the Taught ≥ filter).
