      Data & methodology

      • Grades — official UCSD grade distributions, 2015–2026, aggregated per course × professor across quarters. The bar shows the full spread A/B/C/D/F, plus W (withdraw) and P/NP; Avg GPA is the historical mean.
      • Offered this term — matched against the live Fall 2026 WebReg catalog ( course codes). An Offered tag means the course runs this term.
      • This term's instructor (FA26) — pulled from the WebReg Course Planner catalog (SahirSSharma), which lists each Fall 2026 section's professor. On offered rows we show the actual current instructor(s); a Teaching now tag means the row's past instructor is the one teaching it this term, so their grade history is directly relevant. rows are tagged this way.
      • Schedule builder — add offered courses to My Schedule to lay out lectures/discussions/labs on a weekly calendar (with conflict detection) and see the buildings on a campus map. Section times, rooms and instructors come from the same FA26 catalog; selections are saved in your browser. Export to Calendar downloads an .ics file (Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook) with weekly recurring events for the Fall 2026 term (Sep 24 – Dec 4), campus holidays excluded and Pacific-time DST handled.
      • RMP — RateMyProfessors (school 1079), name-matched (~80% hit). Difficulty lower is easier; again is would-take-again %. Blank means no match.
      • Grad backfill — courses with no grade data (mostly 200+) are filled in reverse from professors' RMP course history, shown as RMP only. Those RMP scores are the professor's overall rating, not specific to that course.
      • Caveat — history is a guide, not a guarantee; instructors and grading policies change. Fewer times taught = less reliable (use the Taught ≥ filter).
